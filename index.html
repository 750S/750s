About Us

750Sparks is a robotics team part of the South Brunsiwck High School robotics program. We have built a robot to participate in the 2018-2019 VEX Robotics competition, Turning Point. There are nine competitors on the 750Sparks team: Karthik Sankar, Nicholas Sahu, Suchit Peddireddy, Tanav Marupudi, Vijay Ramakrishnan, Tejas Iyer, Suhuruth Nelapatla, Jason Louison and Abimanyu Ananthu. Thanks to the Robotics Parents Association and our generous sponsors, the nine of us have been granted the oppurtunity to purchase parts and compete. We will do so fervently and hope to travel all the way to the VEX World Championship this year.
